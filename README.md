# The Stoic Morning 🌅

A guided daily morning ritual app rooted in Stoic philosophy. Walk through all 7 principles every morning with full audio narration, guided timers, reflective journaling, and streak tracking.

## The 7 Steps

1. **The Power of Predictable Ritual** — Build your lighthouse
2. **Win the Battle Before It Begins** — Fortify your mind
3. **The Holy Hour of Silence** — 10-minute guided silence timer
4. **Move With Purpose, Not Pressure** — 5-minute movement timer
5. **Feed Your Mind Before the World Does** — Daily Stoic wisdom passage
6. **Set Your Intention, Not Just Goals** — Daily declaration
7. **Gratitude as Foundation** — Three specific gratitudes

## Features

- 🎧 Full audio narration for every step (Web Speech API)
- ⏱️ Guided timers with audio check-ins
- 📖 14 rotating daily Stoic wisdom passages
- 🔥 Streak tracking & morning history
- 💾 All entries saved locally
- 📱 Mobile-first design
